How to Play

  • Each player takes turns dropping their piece into a column.
  • Player 1's pieces are red, and Player 2's are yellow.
  • The goal is to connect four pieces in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The first player to achieve this wins the game.
  • Click "Reset Game" to start over at any time.
